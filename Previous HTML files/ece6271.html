ECE 6271 - Adaptive Filtering

Summer 2018

The purpose of this course is for you to understand and learn how to use various types of adaptive filters. Adaptive filters are essential component for systems that work under the changing conditions in the real world.

Title

This semester coverage will include:

  • Gradient search algorithms
  • LMS (least mean square) adaptive filters and variations
  • RLS (recursive least square) adaptive filters
  • Kalman filters
  • Particle filters
  • Adaptive Wiener filters
  • ... and applications
